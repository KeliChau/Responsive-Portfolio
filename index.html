About Me

Human. Lifelong Learner. Artist.


Hello! My name is Keli Chau and I'm a photographer and filmmaker from Atlanta, GA. When I'm not shooting, I develop websites. I also run a creative media consulting service developing websites, providing media content, and managing an online presence for business owners. I enjoy learning, trying, and improving anything I'm interested in, but more importantly, I make sure to set aside time for my loved ones.

Thank you for stopping by. You can browse through my galleries, lifestyle blog, designs, projects, and services while you're here. If you would like to touch base for a collaboration, services, or to simply say hello, please feel free to contact me and connect with me on social media.

Cheers,
